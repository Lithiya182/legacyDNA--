# Demo Safe Questions

These questions have been validated against the current LegacyDNA memory system and consistently produce accurate, demo-ready answers.

## Sponsors

* Which sponsor paid earliest across all years?
* Which sponsor should never be re-engaged and why?
* Who is the most reliable anchor sponsor for TechFest?
* How did TechCorp's commitment change year over year from 2023 to 2025?
* Did Global Systems Inc pay on time, and what is their status for 2026?
* What tier of sponsor was CloudNine in 2025?
* How much did TechCorp pay in 2024?

## Attendance & Marketing

* What was the exact attendance for TechFest 2023, 2024, and 2025?
* How did the Student Ambassador program change from 2024 to 2025?
* How many days in advance did marketing launch in 2024?
* Did early marketing correlate with higher attendance?

## Logistics & Operations

* Who was the caterer for TechFest 2025, and how did they perform?
* What was the venue for TechFest 2025?
* Which session drew the largest single-session crowd in TechFest history?
* What happens when we rely on cheap food vendors?
* What happens if we allocate booth space without signed payment confirmation?
* Who should be hired for catering next year?

## Synthesis / Memory Intelligence

* What mistake did we make with StartupX that we should avoid in the future?
* Summarize the top 3 pieces of advice for TechFest 2026.
