Event: TechFest 2025
Date: March 14-16, 2025
Venue: Grand Convocation Hall, Main Campus
Attendance: 4,800 attendees (up from 3,100 in 2024 and 2,400 in 2023)
Status: Completed Successfully

Sponsors:
- TechCorp (Platinum Sponsor, 3rd consecutive year)
- Global Systems Inc (Gold Sponsor, 2nd consecutive year)
- Nova Robotics (Silver Sponsor, new this year)

Highlights:
- Largest attendance in TechFest history, a 55% increase over 2024
- Premium Catering Solutions delivered all three days without a single complaint logged
- Student Ambassador Program (expanded from 12 to 40 members) drove early ticket signups across all departments
- Keynote on applied robotics drew the largest single-session crowd to date
- Zero major logistics incidents reported across the full event

Problems:
- Wi-Fi congestion in the main hall during peak hours on Day 2 (minor, resolved within 40 minutes)
- One workshop room double-booked due to a scheduling spreadsheet error

Decisions Made:
- Confirmed venue and vendor contracts 60 days in advance, compared to roughly 10 days before the 2023 event
- Replaced Campus Bites with Premium Catering Solutions following repeated 2023-2024 food service complaints
- Expanded the Student Ambassador Program based on its success driving 2024 attendance growth
- Introduced a shared scheduling tool to prevent the kind of manual booking errors seen this year

Lessons Learned:
- Early vendor and venue confirmation (60+ days out) directly correlates with smoother execution and higher attendance
- Switching catering vendors after sustained complaints (rather than tolerating repeat issues) eliminates a recurring pain point
- Manual spreadsheet-based room scheduling is no longer reliable at this event size; a shared tool is required going forward
